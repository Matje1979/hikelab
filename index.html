About us

Hike Lab is a non-governmental and non-profit organization located in Serbia dedicated to developing socially beneficial applications and platforms to promote outdoor activities, a healthy lifestyle, and environmental conservation. Through education, connecting enthusiasts, and supporting participants, the organization aims to inspire people of all ages to actively engage in outdoor sports.

Our projects

We are currently developing a website that will serve as a central hub for all public outdoor events organized by outdoor sports enthusiasts from the Balkan region. Additionally, we are creating an engaging quiz designed to educate people in a fun and interactive way about mountaineering and the essential skills for safe outdoor adventures. Lastly, we are launching a challenge to motivate people to walk more than usual over a set period, encouraging an active and healthy lifestyle.

What We Strive For

Our organization aims to foster collaboration with other like-minded entities to jointly develop socially beneficial applications that address community needs. We seek to engage a broad spectrum of individuals, particularly IT professionals and outdoor activities enthusiasts, by creating opportunities for them to contribute their skills and passion. By promoting outdoor activities, we hope to encourage healthier, more active lifestyles while also supporting charitable causes through our efforts. Together, we strive to make a positive impact both in technology and in the world around us.
